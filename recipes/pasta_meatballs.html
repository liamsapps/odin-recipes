Pasta and Meatballs

Description

Hereâ€™s a delicious homemade tomato sauce with meatballs recipe. This will combine juicy, flavorful meatballs with a rich, savory tomato sauce, with whichever pasta you choose to combine it with, making for a perfect comfort meal.

Ingredients

Meatballs

  • 1/2 cup italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Sauce

  • 2 chopped onions
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (796mL or 28 ounce) cans tomatoes
  • 1 (156mL or 5.5 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon chili peppers (or not, you're choice)
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Pasta

This sauce goes great with spaghetti, rigatoni, capellini, or with whichever pasta you prefer.

  • 1 package of pasta (your choice)

Steps

Meatballs

  1. Combine the milk and breadcrumbs. Place the breadcrumbs in a small bowl, pour in the milk, and stir to combine. Set aside while preparing the rest of the meatball mixture. The breadcrumbs will absorb the milk and become soggy.
  2. Whisk the egg, salt, pepper, Parmesan, and parsley. Whisk the egg in a large bowl until broken up. Add the Parmesan, parsley, salt, and a generous quantity of black pepper, and whisk to combine.
  3. Add the ground meat. Add the meat to the egg mixture. Use your hands to thoroughly mix the egg mixture into the ground meat.
  4. Add the onions and soaked breadcrumbs. Add the onions, garlic, and soaked breadcrumbs. Mix them thoroughly into the meat with your fingers. Try not to overwork the meat; pinch the meat between your fingers rather than kneading it.
  5. Form the meat into meatballs. Have a bowl or plate ready. Pinch off a piece of the meat mixture and gently roll between your hands to form 1 1/2-inch meatballs (about 2 tablespoons each). Continue shaping until all the meat is used, placing the meatballs on the dish so that they are not touching.
  6. Place a large pot on medium high heat with a tablespoon of olive oil.
  7. Cook the meatballs, turning frequently, for 5-10 minutes.

Sauce

I like to cook my sauce in the same pot as the meatballs.

  1. Using the same pot with the meatballs, on medium high heat, add the chopped onions and minced garlic.
  2. Let the mixture cook for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Stir in tin tomatoes and tomato paste, as well as, italian seasoning, chili peppers (if desired), and salt. Cover, reduce heat to medium, and cook for 45 minutes. Remember to stir occassionaly!
  4. Reduce to low heat and simmer for an additional 30 minutes.

Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta to the pot.
  3. Follow the instructions provided for the chosen pasta (refer to the package).
  4. Once cooked, drain the pasta

Now you have everything you need for a wonderful meal! Serve hot and enjoy!
